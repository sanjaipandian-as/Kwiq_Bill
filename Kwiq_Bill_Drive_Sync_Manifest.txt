Kwiq Bill - Google Drive Sync Manifest
======================================

The following data categories and events are automatically synchronized to your Google Drive to ensure multi-device consistency and data safety:

1. SALES & INVOICES
-------------------
- Invoice Creation: Full details of every bill generated.
- Invoice Edits: Changes to existing bills (items, amounts, dates).
- Status Updates: Marking bills as Paid, Pending, or Cancelled.
- Invoice Deletion: Removes the record and triggers auto-stock restoration.

2. INVENTORY & PRODUCTS
-----------------------
- Product Creation: Name, Category, Price, Unit, Tax Rate.
- Product Details: Updates to descriptions, barcodes (SKU), and variants.
- Stock Levels: 
    * Real-time deduction on sales.
    * Explicit stock synchronization after every invoice.
    * Low-stock threshold (Min Stock) alerts.
- Product Deletions: Clears product data across all devices.

3. CUSTOMER DATA
----------------
- Profile: Names, Phone numbers, Emails, and Addresses.
- Financials: 
    * Amount Paid: Total lifetime revenue from the customer.
    * Outstanding Dues: Pending balances for credit tracking.
- Loyalty: Loyalty points and customer tags.
- Preferences: WhatsApp and SMS opt-in settings.

4. EXPENSE MANAGEMENT
---------------------
- New Expenses: Titles, categories, payment methods, and timestamps.
- Adjustments: Manual changes to expense records.
- Deletions: Removal of expense logs.

5. SETTINGS & BRANDING
----------------------
- Store Profile: Shop name, Legal name, GSTIN, and Address.
- User Profile: Login details and role.
- Invoice Design: Selected templates, headers, footers, and logo settings.
- Tax Configuration: SGST, CGST, and IGST groups.

6. AUTO-SAVE BACKUPS (Local + Cloud)
-----------------------------------
- Instant JSON exports of your complete Customer, Product, and Invoice tables.
- These are kept as "master copies" in your phone's storage and mirrored to Drive.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Technical Detail:
Data is stored using an "Event Sourcing" model in the 'Kwiqbill/events' folder 
on your Google Drive, ensuring that even if you use multiple phones simultaneously, 
every single action is merged without data loss.
